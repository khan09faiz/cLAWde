## 3. Core Pain Points

1. Lack of legal understanding
2. Fear of uploading sensitive contracts
3. High legal consultation cost
4. Time-consuming manual review
5. No Indian-law-aware AI tools focused on individuals

---

## 4. Our Solution

A **privacy-first AI-powered contract intelligence platform** that:

- Analyzes contracts clause-by-clause
- Detects risks
- Explains in plain English
- Provides negotiation insights
- Supports Indian-law awareness
- Offers secure & optional local inference
